Let's talk Education

CUSEC is a non profit conference event organized annually for students, by students from across Canada. Our mission is to educate and expose students to a diverse range of areas in software engineering and computer science. We empower them to discover knowledgeable speakers, connect with sponsoring companies, and make lifelong friends, all in a safe and comfortable space. We take great pride in hosting unique and knowledgeable speakers that come from many backgrounds in the software industry. Notable past speakers include Alexis Ohanian, Jeff Hodges, Richard Stallman, Julia Evans, and Liz Abinante.

FAQ

Here are some answers to your questions. If we haven't answered your question, feel free to reach out!

When and Where is CUSEC Happening?

CUSEC 2019 will be held in January 2019. More information about the location, schedule and confirmed dates is coming.

How can I get a ticket?

The best way to purchase a ticket will be through the event's page when the tickets go on sale. You can also reach out to the Head Delegate of your University for more information.

When will the schedule be made available?

The schedule will be up a few weeks before the event. We are working around the clock to create the best conference experience for you, promised.

Who can attend?

High school, undergraduate and graduate students from all across Canada can attend. Professionals can also purchase a ticket to attend the event.

How do I get there?

High school, undergraduate and graduate students from all across Canada can attend. Once applications have been reviewed, all students will be notified by email regarding the status of their application.

What are the costs of attending?

We are working hard to reduce the price of the tickets for the event. Information about the price of the tickets for the 2019 edition will be made available shortly, stay tuned.
