What is CUSEC?

CUSEC is a non profit conference event organized annually for students, by students from across Canada. Our mission is to educate and expose students to a diverse range of areas in software engineering and computer science. We empower them to discover knowledgeable speakers, connect with sponsoring companies, and make lifelong friends, all in a safe and comfortable space. We take great pride in hosting unique and knowledgeable speakers that come from many backgrounds in the software industry. Notable past speakers include Alexis Ohanian, Jeff Hodges, Richard Stallman, Julia Evans, and Liz Abinante.

FAQ

Here are some answers to your questions. If we haven't answered your question, feel free to reach out!

When and Where is CUSEC Happening?

CUSEC 2019 will be held on January 10-11-12, at the Marriott Chateau Champlain Hotel in the heart of Montreal. More information about the location and schedule will be made available shortly.

How can I get a ticket?

The best way to purchase a ticket will be through the event's page when the tickets go on sale. There will be three Rounds of tickets for both students and professionals. The 1st round starts October 30th, 2nd November 9th and 3rd November 19th at 7PM EST. You can also reach out to the Head Delegate of your University for more information. Look on the Schools page to find your delegate.

When will the schedule be made available?

The schedule will be up a few weeks before the event. It will be available in the Schedule section of the website. We are working around the clock to create the best conference experience for you, promised.

Can I re-sell my ticket?

It is strictly forbidden to re-sell a CUSEC ticket above the purchased price. If you cannot attend anymore, you can re-sell your ticket at the price you have purchased it, to someone else, or request a refund. Reach out to info@cusec.net if you have any questions. Be advised that a ticket purchased for more than its original value will be cancelled.

Who can attend?

High school, undergraduate and graduate students from all across Canada can attend. Professionals can also purchase a ticket to attend the event. The ticket are now available through the links provided at the top of the website.

How do I get there?

By air, land or sea. You can drive to the venue, although keep in mind that parking in Downtown montreal is limited. Get in touch with your Head Delegate. They are in the best position to tell you if buses are being organized or if carpooling will be available. Many university delegations from outside of Montreal also travel in group, so make sure to join up with them for the best CUSEC experience.

What are the costs of attending?

We have always worked hard to keep the ticket price affordable for students. Discounted student tickets are priced at 60$ per unit. Professional tickets retail at 150$.

How can I get a refund?

Reach out to your Head Delegate or through us at info@cusec.net and we will do our best to assist you. Your Head Delegate can also help you find someone else who would like to come to CUSEC but did not manage to purchase a ticket. We are here to help and don't hesistate to reach out!
