Sponsors, You're Invited.


We would like to invite you to join us at CUSEC 2019, Canada's Annual Software Engineering Conference, hosted annually in the heart of Montreal. The Canadian University Software Engineering Conference (CUSEC) is an annual event that brings students from across the country together to learn, network, and talk about software. Started in 2002 by students with a passion for software, hosted in the heart of Montreal, CUSEC has grown over 16 years and now proudly hosts over 500 students from universities all across the nation. When you attend our event, all of Canada becomes your audience.
